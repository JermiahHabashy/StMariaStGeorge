About us

Welcome to St.Maria & St.George

Santa Maria & Saint George has been active in Lommel since 1996. With his Mediterranean cuisine, Emil has made several generations happy with his freshly prepared dishes.

Emil (owner/cook) has been in the business for more than 30 years and started in Lommel with Saint George in 1996 at Dorp 45, soon a strong connection was made with Lommel and the decision was made not to leave Lommel anymore. In 2005 Emil bought the old bolero to start Santa Maria, a few years later the decision was made to merge Santa Maria and Saint George into one, which eventually became a family restaurant.

Today, Emil is still behind the stove and his wife Mervat welcomes you with all love as a hostess. At St.Maria & St.George, good fresh food, family and friendship are the main focus, and with a large menu there is something for everyone.

~The fondest memories are made gathered around the table~
